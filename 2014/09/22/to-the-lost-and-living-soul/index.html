致远方逝去和未逝的灵魂 to the Lost and Living Soul in South Africa, Far Away From China

By Evan Tao

发表于 2014-09-22

惊闻Sune和Leandi的父亲去世，多年前的的欢乐片段又浮上了脑海。
I was saddened to hear the passing away of Sune and Leandi’s father, the happy time with Flippie and Sannie’s family in South Africa recalled in my head.

逝者已经无可挽回，生者还是要继续自己的生活。
The lost was irrevocable,The living will be continue their life.

我祈祷他在天堂一切都好。
I pray God will take care of him in the heaven for ever.

下面的部分是我几年前写给我失去亲人的朋友的,有些受佛教影响。
The following words was writed to my friend who lost her close relative several years ago, some impacted by Buddhism.

听我说 Published in the Sep 21 2010

I have translated it into English.

##听我说 Listen to me

听我说
Listen to me,

我们都不知道世界的尽头是什么
we all don’t know where is the ending of the world,

也不知道时间是以什么来结尾
don’t know when the time come to end.

我们不知道今世享的福受的罪
We don’t know what mean of happiness and suffering in this world,

在来世意味着什么
and was in the otherworldliness.

或许我们看到听到的感觉到的痛苦
Or the pain we have heared and felt,

只是对来世的历练
was just for the ravages of otherworldliness,

或者说就更本没有来世
Or there was no otherworldliness.

听我说
Listen to me,

谁也不知道在另外的一个世界的生活
No one knows the life in the other world,

我们暂且说叫做生活吧
Let’s assume for a moment that was life,

是开心还是难过
is happy or hard.

当我们还沉浸在离去的痛苦中时
When we are in a fog of sorrow and pain,

说不定对于他是解脱
maybe is moksa for him.

听我说
Listen to me,

魂魄是永生还是消亡？
Was the soul eternal or perished?

我们去了之后才知道
We will know when we been there.

有没有另外的世界，有的话会是什么样子？
Is there another world,what is it look like?

我们去了之后才知道
We will know when we been there.

有没有来生？
Is there otherworldliness?

我们去了之后才知道
We will know when we been there.

Sannie

Sune and Leandi’s mother
